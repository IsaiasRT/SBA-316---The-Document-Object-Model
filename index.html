World of Warcraft: Midnight — Season 1

The Voidstorm Descends Upon Quel'Thalas

Expansion Launch

World of Warcraft: Midnight launched globally on March 2, 2026, raising the level cap to 90 and introducing the long-awaited Player Housing system. Season 1 followed on March 17, bringing the first raid tier of the expansion.

The Story So Far

Xal'atath, the Harbinger, unleashes her void invasion on the heart of the blood elf homelands in Eversong Woods. The looming Voidstorm threatens to extinguish all light and cover Azeroth in darkness. Players must forge new alliances among the light and shadows alike to push back the forces of the Void.

Season 1 Raids

  • Voidspire — A twisted void-corrupted spire in the heart of the Voidstorm. 8 bosses.
  • Dreamrift — A tear in reality where the Void meets the Emerald Dream. 4 bosses.
  • March on Quel'Danas — The climactic assault on the sacred isle to push back Xal'atath's forces. 5 bosses.

New & Returning Dungeons

Mythic+ Season 1 features eight dungeons: Magister's Terrace, Maisara Caverns, Nexus Point Xenas, Windrunner Spire, plus legacy dungeons Pit of Saron, Seat of the Triumvirate, Skyreach, and Algeth'ar Academy.

New Features

Player Housing with neighborhood districts, the Devourer Demon Hunter specialization (mid-range Void DPS), redesigned Silvermoon City as a shared Horde/Alliance hub, and built-in UI tools including a damage meter and boss timers.

Patch 12.0.5

Lingering Shadows — Now Live

Released April 21, 2026

Patch 12.0.5 introduces Void Assaults, open-world events where players defend key points across Midnight from Xal'atath's forces. Disrupt powerful Ritual Sites, earn Nebulous & Ascendant Voidcores through the new Voidforge system, and compete in Decor Duels — a festive housing decoration contest. Also includes class tuning adjustments and the Abyss Anglers fishing activity.
